BUSINESS ADVANTAGE PROPERTY POLICY

ABC Manufacturing Ltd. has purchased this insurance to safeguard its business assets against unexpected events.

The company building is insured for a maximum amount of $1,250,000. Office equipment, computers, furniture, and inventory are collectively protected under business personal property coverage with a policy limit of $350,000.

If normal operations are interrupted because of a covered loss, business income protection provides reimbursement of up to $250,000 until operations resume. Equipment breakdown protection is included with a maximum benefit of $120,000, while debris removal expenses are covered up to $40,000.

This insurance is intended to protect against accidental events only. Losses caused by pollution, cyber attacks, fraudulent employee activities, acts of war, or confiscation by government authorities are specifically excluded from coverage.

Any claim must be reported within 30 days of the occurrence together with supporting documentation.
